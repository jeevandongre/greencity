Problem Statement

  • Very few organizations provide pickup/drop service to their employees.
  • Employees without office commute facilities have difficulty in using inefficient public transport, thereby increasing the use of personal and private vehicles.
  • Congestion on roads is increased by usage of non-shared transport increasing stress & decreasing productivity.
  • Western concepts such as car pooling have proven ineffective in the Indian environment.
  • Pollution is a great challenge in Indian metros’ and petrol is the valuable resource which we want to save.

Indicates clear demand for efficient commute solution targeting office goers

Proposed Solution

  • Create “Commute market place” where
    • Regular office commuters express intent to commute with daily commute details like pickup/drop points and time.
    • Travel vendors provide customized commute solutions based on the expressed interest (time, source/destination etc.)
    • Commuters with similar interests are aggregated and matched to an interested vendor
  • Benefits for commuters
    • Stress free, economic travel in optimal time.
  • Benefits for Vendors
    • New source of revenues
  • Benefits for the ecosystem
    • Reduce pollution & congestion

Deep dive

  • Commuter subscribes via online registration form, providing daily commute details.
  • Correspondingly, travel vendor registers with details of available inventory, price expectations, preferred timings & routes.
    • Vendors will also need verification in terms of PAN no., KST / CST no.
  • Travel vendor will login and query the list of people interested for the given source / destination / time.
  • Based on the number of commuters, travel vendor will compute the vehicle size and sends monthly / quarterly / half yearly subscription fee details to commuters. (online)
  • Vendors will contact commuters and decide payment terms and schedule. (offline)
  • Commuters will confirm vendors online to avoid spam.

Incentives to Y!

  • ECOMARC certification
    • This initiative provides an opportunity to reduce the petrol consumption and in turn reducing the pollution level.
    • Significant adoption of the scheme by commuters could allow Yahoo! could get ECOMARC stamping from the Govt. of India.
  • Integration possibilities exist with other properties - Our City, Yahoo Maps, Yahoo Groups and other communities.
  • Opportunity for smaller businesses in India to move online and cross-selling opportunities (e.g. Small Business) will open up.

Architecture/Post launch requirements

  • Yapache with mysql/php support
  • Bulkmail support
  • Y! login
  • Travel vendor registration requests should be validated manually to avoid spams.
  • Y! Map API’s

Challenges/Limitations – How to Address

  • Once user subscribe to a specific travel vendor he/she might not come back to the site. So, might not drive much traffic into the site.

    -  Allow commuters to subscribe one time trips as and when the need arise. Make travel vendors to come up with optimistic dynamic pools for onetime trips.

    -  Show the price comparisons to commuters.

  • Initial adoption of travel vendors and commuters.

     -  Email campaign

     -  Provide first year free subscription to travel vendors

     -  Provide links from myCity, Y! India and other sites.

  • Registration process

    - Manually someone has to allow/disallow the travel vendor registration request to avoid spams from substandard vendors.

Pre launch

  • Invite travel vendors and extract the seed data like

    - Price for the specific routes with pool size and logistics.

    - Publish the monthly subscription prices with different pooled services

    - Give comparison against car/auto/taxi/two wheeler.

  • Promote it to organizations where commute is not provided. (PR)

Business Model/Opportunities

  • Commuters can freely subscribe
  • Travel vendors has to pay the annual subscription fee
  • Additional ad service to travel vendors to place about their organization/pooled weekend trips/customized trips.
  • Potential candidate to integrate with other yahoo properties like Our City, Yahoo Maps, Yahoo Travel and other communities.
